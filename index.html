What is Tae Kwon Do?

It is a Korean-style of martial arts, which literally means the foot, the fist, and the way.
It is an art which dates back more than 2,000 years.
Aside from the aspects of self-defense and competition, Tae Kwon Do teaches students the values of respect, discipline, confidence, and personal development.
